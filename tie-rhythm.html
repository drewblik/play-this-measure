The trick to ties

A tie glues two noteheads of the same pitch into one sound: strike once, hold for the combined length. A tied-into notehead is drawn hollow blue — written, but never re-pressed. The last example shows the tricky case: a tied note you keep holding while new notes are struck on top of it.
